Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SoftDrinks.

Do you deliver nationwide?

Yes! We deliver to all major cities across Pakistan. Same-day delivery is available within city limits for orders placed before 5 PM.

What is your return policy?

We offer hassle-free returns within 48 hours of delivery. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team and we'll arrange a full refund or replacement.

Are all your products authentic?

Absolutely! We source directly from authorized brand distributors. Every product comes with a freshness guarantee and authenticity seal.

What are the payment options?

We accept cash on delivery, JazzCash, EasyPaisa, bank transfers, and all major credit/debit cards through our secure payment gateway.

Is there a minimum order amount?

There is no minimum order. However, orders above Rs. 999 qualify for free delivery. Bundle orders of 12+ drinks get up to 25% off automatically.
